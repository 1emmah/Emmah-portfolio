Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 eggs

Instructions

  1. Boil water and cook spaghetti.
  2. Cook pancetta in a pan.
  3. Mix eggs and cheese, combine with pasta.

Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients

  • 100g dark chocolate
  • 100g butter
  • 2 eggs

Instructions

  1. Melt chocolate and butter.
  2. Whisk eggs and sugar, then combine with chocolate mixture.
  3. Bake at 200°C for 10-12 minutes.
